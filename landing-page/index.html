MWN My Writing Notebook

  • Write Your Way

    Your writing is a personal thing. Your process. Your words organized your way. We won't tell you how to store your work.

  • Write Everywhere

    Work online or offline. On your tablet or mobile device. On your desktop.

  • Write Privately (or not)

    Your workspace is just that yours. You can keep it all to yourself, or share with a team. Share only one project, or all projects, it's up to you.

  • Markdown Formatting

    Format your documents using markdown. Easy to read. Easy to format. No HTML required.

  • Meta Data

    Add document meta to data to pages. Do you always have a PoV character on each scene? make a meta data. Keep track of characters in a scene. Whatever you use regularly can become meta data.

  • Comments

    It's not just enough to have others read your writing, the ability to get feedback is important. Comment on projects, folders or individual pages.
